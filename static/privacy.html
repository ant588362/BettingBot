Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

1. Who We Are

Sports Circuit Picks ("we," "us," or "our") operates a subscription-based sports analysis service accessible through Whop. Our service provides AI-generated sports picks and analysis for informational and entertainment purposes.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information directly. Specifically:

  • Account information — Your subscription and payment details are managed entirely by Whop. We do not store your payment information.
  • Usage data — When you use the AI Chat, your questions are sent to our server and processed by Anthropic's Claude API to generate responses. We do not permanently store chat messages.
  • Technical data — Standard server logs (IP address, browser type, timestamps) for operational purposes. Logs are retained for up to 30 days.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the picks and AI chat service
  • To monitor for abuse and enforce rate limits
  • To diagnose technical issues

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

Our service uses the following third-party providers:

  • Whop — Subscription management, payments, and identity. Subject to Whop's Privacy Policy.
  • Anthropic (Claude API) — Powers the AI chat analysis. Subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • The Odds API — Provides live sports odds data. No personal information is shared with this service.
  • Railway — Cloud hosting provider. Subject to Railway's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your information. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure. By using our service you acknowledge this inherent risk.

6. Children's Privacy

Our service is intended for users aged 18 or older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has used our service, contact us immediately.

7. Your Rights

You may request deletion of any personal data we hold by contacting us. Since most account data is held by Whop, you may also manage your data through your Whop account settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
